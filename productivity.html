Productivity, Social Interaction & Mental Health

Productivity - From the words of our CIC Leader for the EMEA, Harry Olaru, said that once the pandemic hit they excpected a huge drop in productivity and deliverance, as did our clients. But no. Productivity had even seemed to increase as people adjusted to their new working environments. Now however, this rate has seen a drop and many IBMers are saying that there are too many distractions at home, and the office is actually the perfect place to get their work done.
Social Interaction - Another aspect which we are hearing loads and loads about is how people after spending such an extended period of time, in their homes, not being allowed ot go out and socialise, interact as they usually do, really is something that was lost when they stopped coming into the office, the one place they would come in without fail each and every day, and so they miss that and want that back, for their productivity, but also their own sanity!
